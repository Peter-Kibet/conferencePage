Optimus Cage Defender Logo and Company Information

The logo of Optimus Cage Defender is a shield with a cage in the middle. The cage is a metaphor for the "Not personal info outlet" and The shield is protecting the cage from the outside world.

The logo competition, a celebration of creativity and innovation, concluded on May 20th this year. We were thrilled to witness the exceptional talent and ingenuity displayed by aspiring graphic designers. Amongst the outstanding entries, Peter Kibet's submission stood out, captivating hearts with its remarkable concept and exquisite execution. Peter's artistic prowess serves as an inspiration to young graphic designers, reminding them of the limitless possibilities that lie within their imagination. Embrace your unique vision, unleash your creativity, and let your designs paint a vibrant world of inspiration and storytelling. The future of graphic design rests in your hands!
